Whole Grain Dark Chocolate Chip Cookies

A timeless favorite, made with whole grain flour and dark chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole grain flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until creamy.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the butter mixture and beat until smooth.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until combined.
  6. Fold in the dark chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop spoonfuls of the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Let cool on a wire rack before serving.
